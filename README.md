## Frequently Asked Questions

### I don't hear any music or sound effects
1. Check your iPhone's physical mute switch / Silent Mode.
2. Open the in-game **Settings** panel (from the title screen or the in-game pause menu) and confirm **Music**, **Sound effects**, and **Vibration** are toggled **ON**.

### How do I turn off aim assist?
Settings → **Aim assist** → toggle off. It's a simple on/off switch — there's no strength slider.

### How do I reset my progress?
Settings → **Reset progress**. You'll be asked to tap a second time to confirm — this also clears your leaderboard history, so make sure that's what you want before confirming.

### How do I replay the tutorial?
Settings → **Replay tutorial**.

### Which devices does Skeet Golf support?
iPhone only, running a recent iOS version. The game requires a gyroscope (every iPhone has one), so it isn't available on iPad or Apple Vision Pro.

### Is Skeet Golf free? Are there ads or in-app purchases?
Skeet Golf is completely free with no ads and no in-app purchases.

### What data does Skeet Golf collect?
None. See the [Privacy Policy](./PRIVACY.md) for details — everything (scores, settings, progress) stays on your device.
