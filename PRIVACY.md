# Skeet Golf — Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** July 1, 2026

Skeet Golf ("the app," "we," "us") does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, from anyone, including children.

## Summary

- No accounts, sign-in, or personal information of any kind is requested or required.
- No analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are included in the app.
- No data is sent to us, to Apple, or to any third party.
- Everything the app remembers about you stays on your device.

## What the app stores, and where

Skeet Golf saves your game progress, scores, round history, and settings (music/sound/vibration/aim-assist toggles) locally on your device using Apple's standard `UserDefaults` storage. This data:

- Never leaves your device.
- Is not backed up to any server we control (it may be included in your device's own iCloud/iTunes backup, which is managed entirely by Apple and your Apple Account, not by us).
- Can be permanently erased at any time via **Settings → Reset progress** inside the app, or by deleting the app.

## Device sensors

Skeet Golf uses your iPhone's gyroscope (via Apple's Core Motion framework) to let you aim by tilting your phone. This motion data is processed in real time, on your device, purely to control the aiming reticle. It is never recorded, stored, or transmitted anywhere.

## Children's privacy

Because Skeet Golf does not collect any personal data from any user, it does not knowingly collect personal data from children, and no additional data practices apply based on age.

## Third parties

Skeet Golf does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-sharing services. No data is shared with third parties because no data is collected in the first place.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes — for example, if a future version of the app adds an online leaderboard or other networked feature — this page will be updated with a new effective date before that version ships, and the change will be described here.
